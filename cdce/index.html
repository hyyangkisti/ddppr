COVID-19 Drug Repurposing Evaluation
(PK Prediction)

The drug repurposing for COVID-19 is evaluated as aspect of prediction of human pharmacokinetics in comparison with IC50. This study is done by CNU, KRIBB and KISTI (2021). The candidate drugs close to COVID-19 are extracted by machine learning. If you want to know about the whole drug list of Disease Drug Protein Potential Relationship based on machine learning analysis, see this page.
